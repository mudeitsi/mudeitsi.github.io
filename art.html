Art Gallery

A selection of original works by Minayo Mudeizi Esere. Each piece explores inner worlds, emotion, and perception through colour, contrast, and symbolism. Click through or hover to read a short description of the story behind each canvas.

The Pull Within

2025  •  Mixed media on canvas  •  60 × 80 cm

A surreal self-portrait capturing the tension between past and future selves. Fragmented forms and layered colour fields show the invisible forces pulling a woman toward the life she is meant to live.

Surrealism Inner journey Feminine power

The Price of Vision

2025  •  Acrylic on canvas  •  50 × 70 cm

A portrait of a Kenyan man whose sight is covered by a thousand-shilling note. The piece questions what we sacrifice when money becomes our only lens for seeing the world.

Social commentary Portrait Kenya

Led by the Heart

2025  •  Acrylic & ink  •  50 × 60 cm

A poetic image of a man cleansing a brain with water flowing from a heart, with a clock floating in the background. Logic, intuition, and time meet in one ritual of surrender and renewal.

Symbolism Intuition Time

The Pull of Becoming

2025  •  Mixed media  •  60 × 80 cm

Dual tones and mirrored silhouettes show a woman standing between who she was taught to be and who she truly is. The piece honours the discomfort and beauty of transformation.

Identity Duality

Roar of Return

2025  •  Acrylic on canvas  •  60 × 80 cm

A lion and a woman share one gaze, bathed in warm gold. This work celebrates courage, self-trust, and the moment you finally return to your own voice.

Leo energy Courage
