A selection of original works by Minayo Mudeizi Esere. Each piece explores inner worlds, emotion, and perception through colour, contrast, and symbolism. Click through or hover to read a short description of the story behind each canvas.

Pull Within

2025  •  Mixed media on canvas  •  4 ft × 4 ft (121.9 × 121.9 cm)

A surreal portrayal of an African woman held between shadow and light as multiple inner selves pull at her from behind. The work explores inner conflict, self recognition, and the tension between who one has been and who one is becoming.

Contemporary Art Identity Human Experience

Price of Vision

2025  •  Acrylic on canvas  •  100 × 50 cm (3.28 × 1.64 ft)

A striking portrait of a Kenyan man whose eyes are covered by a thousand shilling note. The work examines how economic systems influence perception, obedience, and fear, questioning what is lost when money becomes the primary lens through which reality is viewed.

Contemporary Art Perception Cultural Dialogue

Lead from the Heart

2025  •  Acrylic & ink  •  4 ft × 4 ft (121.9 × 121.9 cm)

A young African man kneels at the shoreline, cleansing a human brain with water flowing from a glowing heart. The work centers intuition, emotional intelligence, and inner leadership, presenting the heart as a guide that softens thought and restores balance between intellect and embodied knowing.

Roar of Return

2025  •  Acrylic on canvas  •  4 ft × 4 ft (121.9 × 121.9 cm)

A woman and a lion meet in a shared gaze, representing the moment of reclaiming self trust and personal authority. The work speaks to courage, remembrance, and the return to one’s authentic voice after periods of suppression or self doubt.
